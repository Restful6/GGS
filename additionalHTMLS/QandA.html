Important Notice

Questions We Won't Answer

We welcome honest questions and constructive criticism. However, messages that fall into the following categories will be ignored without response:

  • Hostile, aggressive, or confrontational tone
  • Profanity, slurs, or abusive language
  • Personal attacks on team members
  • Spam, self-promotion, or unrelated content
  • Threats, harassment, or intimidation
  • Deliberately misleading or trolling questions
  • Demands for confidential business information
  • Repeated identical messages (spam)

We believe in open communication, but we also believe in basic respect. If you wouldn't say it to someone's face, don't send it. We reserve the right to ignore any message that violates these guidelines.
