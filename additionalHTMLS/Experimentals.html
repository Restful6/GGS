Artificial Intelligence In Development

NEXIS AI

Advanced artificial intelligence system currently in development to redefine interaction in games.

NEXIS AI is an advanced artificial intelligence platform currently being developed by Greek Games Studios. This ambitious project aims to enhance the gaming experience with:

  • Adaptive learning process that responds to play style (planned)
  • Dynamic responses in real time (under development)
  • Personalized experience for each user (in design phase)
  • Integration with all game media (planned)

This technology is currently in early development phase. We're working on the core architecture and expect to begin testing in the coming months.

Our team is excited about the potential of NEXIS AI to transform how players interact with our games, but there's still significant work ahead before it's ready for implementation.

Advertising In Development

Advertising Platform

Customized advertising platform currently in development to integrate seamlessly into the gaming experience.

The Greek Games Studios advertising platform is currently in development with the goal of creating a better experience for both users and advertisers:

  • Personalized ads based on gameplay (in development)
  • Non-intrusive experience without interruptions (planned)
  • Reward system for watching ads (design phase)
  • Full content control by our team (under development)

This technology is currently in the architecture design phase. We're building the foundational systems and expect to begin internal testing later this year.

Our vision is to create an advertising ecosystem that respects players' experience while providing value to developers, but we're still in the early stages of making this a reality.

We're exploring innovative approaches to make ads feel like a natural part of the gaming experience rather than an interruption.
