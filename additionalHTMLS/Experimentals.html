Artificial Intelligence In Development

Project AI

An AI assisted toolset currently being tested internally to help streamline NPC behavior scripting and dialogue generation.

Project AI is an internal AI assisted development tool currently in early testing at Greek Games Studios. It is not a finished product and its capabilities are limited at this stage. Current scope includes:

  • Basic NPC behavior pattern suggestions for prototype builds
  • Assistance with repetitive dialogue drafting (requires heavy manual review)
  • Internal testing only, not integrated into any live game
  • No confirmed release timeline; may be scaled down or discontinued

This project is in a very early experimental phase. Most features are theoretical or prototype level. We are evaluating whether it provides enough value to continue development.

We are being realistic: this is a side experiment, not a flagship technology. Any public release, if it happens at all, is likely years away.

Advertising In Development

Advertising Platform

A basic ad integration framework being explored to support free-to-play titles without ruining the player experience.

We are exploring a lightweight ad framework that could support future free-to-play releases. This is not a custom ad network; it would likely integrate existing solutions with minor customization. Current considerations:

  • Optional rewarded video ads (watch ad, get in-game currency)
  • No forced interstitials between levels, only player-initiated
  • Integration with established ad providers (Google, Unity) rather than building from scratch
  • Strict content filtering to avoid inappropriate ads

This platform is in the research phase. We have not committed to implementing it in any specific game. If we proceed, it will be limited to one or two titles as a test.

We are aware that badly implemented ads destroy player trust. If we cannot do this respectfully, we will not do it at all. No timeline is set. This is a back-burner project that we revisit occasionally.

Merchandise Planned

Studio Merch Store

A small collection of branded merchandise to help support development and give fans a way to represent the studio.

We are planning a limited merchandise run to help fund ongoing development and give back to our community. This is not a full e-commerce operation, just a small curated drop. Planned items include:

  • Studio logo t-shirts and hoodies (black and dark gray variants)
  • Game-themed keychains and enamel pins
  • Mousepads with artwork from our titles
  • Stickers and posters (limited print runs)

This store is not yet live. We are currently researching print-on-demand partners to avoid holding inventory. If costs are too high, we may cancel this idea entirely.

All proceeds, if any, will go directly toward development costs. This is not a profit center; it is a way for supporters to contribute and get something tangible in return. Expected launch: when we make better games, find the right partners, and grow our fanbase. No fixed timeline.
