About the App

GGS Hub is the official companion app for Greek Games Studios, designed to bring the full studio experience to your mobile device. Stay connected with everything GGS has to offer from breaking news and game releases to exclusive Play & Demos and competitive global leaderboards.

Whether you're tracking your high scores, trying out new demos before anyone else, or catching up on the latest announcements, GGS Hub puts the entire studio at your fingertips.
