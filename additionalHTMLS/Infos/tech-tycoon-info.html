Official Guide

Welcome to Tech Company Tycoon! This guide will walk you through the different phases of building your tech empire, from humble beginnings as a game developer to becoming a leader in robotics and AI.

Goal

Build your own tech empire starting from scratch! Evolve from making simple video games to creating powerful devices, consoles, cars, and advanced robots. Manage resources, make smart investments, and dominate the tech world!

Phase 1: Video Game Startup

What You Do:

  • Create and sell mobile/PC games
  • Choose genre, platform, and features (story, graphics, multiplayer)
  • Manage budget, hire your first employee(s)

Tips:

  • Focus on 1 genre to build a fanbase
  • Invest early in marketing upgrades

Phase 2: Device Development

Unlocks when: You reach $X revenue from games

What You Do:

  • Design and launch your own smartphones, tablets, or smartwatches
  • Balance specs (battery, CPU, screen) vs. cost
  • Compete in tech reviews and sales

Tips:

  • Watch trends (e.g., people want fast charging or camera quality)
  • R&D upgrades unlock better components

Phase 3: Console Production

What You Do:

  • Create gaming consoles (portable, home, hybrid)
  • Decide OS, controller design, performance
  • Build exclusive games for your console or sign third-party devs

Tips:

  • Launch your console with a strong lineup of exclusive titles
  • Don't copy others — innovate

Phase 4: Cars Division

What You Do:

  • Enter the smart car market
  • Design electric cars with tech features (autopilot, smart dashboard)
  • Compete with futuristic brands

Tips:

  • Speed, battery life & safety affect car reviews
  • Car factories require heavy investment

Phase 5: Robotics & AI

What You Do:

  • Research & develop humanoid or industrial robots
  • Automate tasks, sell to companies or use them internally
  • Launch your AI system

Tips:

  • Robots can boost your own company's productivity
  • Ethical decisions may affect public opinion

General Tips

  • Manage your money. Don't expand too fast
  • Upgrade departments like Marketing, R&D, HR
  • Watch the market. Compete with AI rival companies
  • Take risks, but smart ones — timing is everything

About the Game

Tech Company Tycoon is an immersive business simulation game where you build a technology empire from the ground up. Start as a small game development studio and gradually expand into hardware, automotive, and robotics industries.

Make strategic decisions about product development, marketing, research, and human resources as you compete in dynamic markets. Balance short-term profits with long-term investments to outmaneuver your competitors.

With multiple paths to success and endless replayability, Tech Company Tycoon offers a deep yet accessible business simulation experience for both casual players and tycoon game enthusiasts.

Key Features

Five Industry Phases

Deep Customization

Dynamic Economy

AI Competitors

Research & Innovation
