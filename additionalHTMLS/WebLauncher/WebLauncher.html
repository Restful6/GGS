What is Play & Demos?

Play & Demos is our interactive web gaming platform where you can experience our games directly in your browser. No downloads, no installations, just instant gaming fun!

This platform showcases mini games, early demos, prototypes, and experimental projects from Greek Games Studios. It's the perfect place to try out new concepts and provide feedback before our games are officially released.
