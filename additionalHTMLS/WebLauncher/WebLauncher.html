What is Play & Demos?

Play & Demos is our interactive web gaming platform where you can experience our games directly in your browser. No downloads, no installations, just instant gaming fun!

This platform showcases mini games, early demos, prototypes and experimental projects from Greek Games Studios. It's the perfect place to try out new concepts and provide feedback before our games are officially released.

Unified Game Launcher

We're currently developing a comprehensive game launcher that will provide a unified experience for all our web games. The launcher will feature game library management, automatic updates, achievements system, and cross-platform compatibility.

Expected features include:

  • Centralized game library with filtering and search
  • Automatic updates and patch management
  • Achievements and progress tracking
  • Cross-platform cloud saves
  • Friend system and multiplayer matchmaking
  • Customizable game settings and controls
