Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Greek Games Studios ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users across all platforms and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in relation to our games, apps, websites, and community services.

This policy applies to:

  • Main Games: Products available on platforms such as the Google Play Store and Steam
  • Mini Games: Experiences on Roblox, Minecraft, and similar platforms
  • Official Website: https://greekgamesstudios.com
  • Other Services: Including testing environments, Discord bots, servers, and digital tools

2. Data Collection

We do not actively collect personal data such as names, emails, or addresses directly from users. However, limited non-personal or platform-based data may be recorded automatically for essential functionality.

a. Gameplay Logs and Usernames

Player usernames, user IDs, and activity logs may be stored for analytics, moderation, or debugging.

These may include error reports, actions taken in-game, or account-level information provided by the platform (e.g., Roblox User ID).

b. Testing and QA

Users may participate in testing phases, knowingly or unknowingly, as part of early releases. We may monitor interactions to improve quality and balance.

Only platform-available identifiers (e.g., usernames) are referenced; no personal data is collected.

c. Moderation and Bans

In the case of rule violations, ban data and related logs may be stored.

This data may include timestamps, offense type, and moderation actions taken.

Certain moderation data may be shared with platform providers when necessary (e.g., Roblox or Discord).

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on external services to operate certain features. These platforms may collect data under their own terms:

  • Roblox Corporation
  • Mojang/Microsoft (Minecraft)
  • Google (Play Store, AdMob, Firebase)
  • Valve (Steam)
  • Wix.com
  • Discord (Bots, community servers)

Please refer to each platform's privacy policy for more details.

4. Use of Data

Any data we collect or access is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving gameplay experience and stability
  • Fixing bugs and preventing exploits
  • Moderating communities and enforcing rules
  • Analyzing trends for development
  • Ensuring platform compliance

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website (hosted via Wix.com) may use cookies or similar technologies for:

  • Functional purposes (navigation, login)
  • Analytics and traffic measurement
  • Experience customization

Users may manage cookie settings through their browsers.

6. Age Requirements

Our Services are intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 13.

If we learn that we've unintentionally collected such data, we will delete it as quickly as possible. Parents may contact us to request deletion.

7. External Links

Our Services may contain links to external websites, stores, or platforms. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. All changes will be posted on our website and reflected by the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of our Services after updates indicates acceptance of the revised terms.

9. Payments and In-Game Purchases

Some of our Services include paid features, such as:

  • Full game purchases (via Steam or Google Play)
  • In-game or in-app purchases (e.g., virtual currency, game passes, cosmetic items)
  • Donation-based features (e.g., Discord perks)

a. Payment Processing

All payments are handled by third-party platforms. We do not store or process billing data (e.g., credit cards, banking info).

Refer to the relevant platform for terms:

  • Google Payments
  • Steam Subscriber Agreement
  • Roblox Terms

b. Virtual Goods & Refunds

Virtual items or currency are non-refundable and non-transferable, unless explicitly stated.

Abuse of payment systems may lead to bans or restricted access.

c. Testing/Promo Access

Players may be granted free or early access to premium content during tests or events.

We reserve the right to remove this content at any time without notice or compensation.
