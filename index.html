Play & Demos v2: Major Update!

We are thrilled to announce the release of Play & Demos v2 a massive update packed with optimizations, reworks, and brand new mini games!

This update focuses on improving performance, fixing various issues and expanding the experience with more content and features.

What's new in v2:

  • Optimizations: Faster loading times and smoother gameplay across all mini games.
  • Reworks: Several existing mini games have been redesigned with better mechanics, improved visuals, and additional features.
  • New Mini Games: We've added 5 brand new titles to the Play & Demos library, each offering unique challenges and fun experiences.
  • Bug Fixes: Addressed stability issues and resolved physics control bugs affecting gameplay responsiveness.

This is just the beginning, we will continue to support and expand Play & Demos with regular updates. Your feedback helps us grow, so don't hesitate to share your thoughts.

October 1, 2025

Development Update: Monetization & Delays

At Greek Games Studios, we are currently working on an important new step for our games: implementing in-app monetization.

This means that players will soon be able to experience our games not only as free releases but also with new ways to support our work and access extra features.

However, while setting this up we have encountered several technical issues, including Gradle failures, permissions, and other unexpected obstacles.

Because of these challenges, the final monetization update for Try Not To Rage: Forest, the release of Try Not To Rage: Sky, as well as other projects in progress will unfortunately face delays.

As a small team, we cannot make everything happen overnight, but we are working every single day to solve these problems and move forward.

We sincerely apologize for these delays and thank you for your patience and continued support.
