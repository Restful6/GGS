Support Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install your games?

Our games are available across multiple platforms. For PC titles, you can download the installer or launcher directly from our official website and follow the simple setup process. Mobile games like Try Not To Rage: Forest can be downloaded from the Google Play Store. iOS support is planned for the future.

What are the system requirements?

System requirements depend on the game. Our mobile games are optimized to run smoothly on most modern Android devices. For PC games, we always aim to balance performance and quality—just check the game’s page for detailed specs before downloading.

How can I report a bug?

If you encounter a bug, you can report it through the in-game support menu (where available) or by filling out the bug report form on our website. Please include as much detail as possible, like what you were doing, any error messages, and screenshots if available.

Where can I find my purchase history?

For mobile games, your purchase history is available through your Google Play account. For PC games, we currently don’t support account-based purchases — but all download and license info is sent directly to your email after purchase. If you need help, feel free to contact our support team.
