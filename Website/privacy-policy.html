Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Greek Games Studios ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users across all our platforms and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our games, apps, websites, and community services.

This policy applies to:

  • Main Games: Titles available on platforms such as Google Play Store and Steam
  • Mini Games: Experiences on Roblox, Minecraft, and similar platforms
  • Official Website: https://greekgamesstudios.com
  • Other Services: Including testing environments, Discord bots, servers, and digital tools

2. Data Collection

We do not actively collect personal data such as names, emails, or addresses directly from users during normal gameplay or website browsing. However, some limited non-personal or platform-based data may be automatically collected to ensure proper functionality.

a. Gameplay Logs and Usernames

Player usernames, user IDs, and gameplay logs may be stored for analytics, moderation, or debugging.

This may include:

  • Error reports
  • In-game actions
  • Account-level information provided by the platform (e.g., Roblox User ID)

b. Testing and QA

Users may participate in testing phases (public or private) as part of early releases. We may monitor gameplay behavior and interactions to improve game balance and performance.

Only platform-provided identifiers (like usernames) are used, no personal data is collected.

c. Moderation and Bans

If a user violates community or platform rules, ban or moderation data may be stored. This may include timestamps, offense type, and actions taken.

In some cases, moderation data may be shared with the relevant platform provider (e.g., Roblox, Discord).

d. Contacting Us (Support or "Message Us")

If you contact us through a support form, email, or "Message Us" feature on our website, we may temporarily store your email address and message. This allows us to:

  • Respond to your request
  • Provide assistance or technical support

We only store this information for as long as needed to resolve your issue. We do not use it for marketing, and we do not share it with third parties.

Some IP information may appear automatically through our hosting provider (Netlify) when you submit a form or visit our website. For how IP addresses are handled or stored, please review Netlify's Privacy Policy.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on external platforms and providers to operate certain services. Each platform manages its own data collection and privacy policies.

These may include:

  • Roblox Corporation
  • Mojang / Microsoft (Minecraft)
  • Google (Play Store, AdMob, Firebase)
  • Valve (Steam)
  • Discord (Bots, servers)
  • Netlify (Website hosting)

Please refer to each provider's privacy policy for more information.

4. Use of Data

Any data we collect or access is used only for:

  • Improving gameplay and stability
  • Fixing bugs and preventing exploits
  • Moderating communities and enforcing rules
  • Analyzing trends for development
  • Ensuring compliance with platform terms

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data under any circumstances.

5. Data Retention

Gameplay, testing, and moderation data are stored for a maximum of 12 months, unless needed for ongoing analysis or moderation. After that period, data is securely deleted.

Support messages and emails are deleted once the issue or request has been resolved.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies for:

  • Functional purposes (navigation, login)
  • Analytics and traffic measurement
  • Improving user experience

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Age Requirements

Our services are designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If such data is ever collected unintentionally, it will be deleted immediately upon discovery. Parents or guardians may contact us to request deletion.

8. User Rights and Requests

Users may request access to or deletion of specific data linked to their activity (e.g., usernames, moderation logs, support messages). Requests must include proof of identity and will be handled within 30 days, where applicable.

9. External Links

Our services may contain links to external websites, stores, or platforms. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external services.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on our official website with the "Last Updated" date.

Continuing to use our services after changes means you accept the updated terms.

11. Payments and In-Game Purchases

Some of our games and apps may include paid features such as:

  • Full game purchases (Steam, Google Play)
  • In-game or in-app purchases (virtual currency, passes, cosmetics)
  • Donation-based perks (e.g., Discord features)

a. Payment Processing

All payments are handled by third-party platforms. We do not store or process billing data (such as credit card or banking information).

Refer to each platform's terms for details:

  • Google Payments
  • Steam Subscriber Agreement
  • Roblox Terms

b. Virtual Goods & Refunds

Virtual items and currency are non-refundable and non-transferable, unless stated otherwise.

Abuse of payment systems may result in bans or restricted access.

c. Testing/Promo Access

Some players may receive early or free access to premium content during tests or events.

We reserve the right to remove or modify this content at any time without prior notice or compensation.

Community Guidelines

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the community of Greek Games Studios (“we”, “our”, or “us”). Our goal is to maintain a safe, positive, and respectful environment for everyone who enjoys our games, websites, and community spaces.

These Community Guidelines apply to all of our platforms and services, including:

  • Games and apps on Roblox, Steam, Google Play, and other platforms
  • Official Website: https://greekgamesstudios.com
  • Discord Servers, Forums, and Testing Environments

By participating in our community, you agree to follow these rules to ensure a respectful and fun experience for everyone.

2. Be Respectful

Treat all players, staff, and community members with respect. Harassment, bullying, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind are not tolerated.

Please avoid:

  • Hate speech, racism, sexism, or discrimination
  • Insults or targeted harassment
  • Sharing personal information of others (doxxing)
  • Toxic behavior, spam, or deliberate provocation

We believe in kindness and fair play, let's keep it that way.

3. Fair Play and Honesty

Everyone deserves a fair gaming experience. You must not:

  • Use cheats, exploits, or unauthorized modifications
  • Attempt to hack, glitch, or manipulate game systems
  • Duplicate or share exploits with others
  • Use third-party software that affects gameplay

Players found exploiting or cheating may face temporary or permanent bans.

4. Safe and Appropriate Content

When communicating in our games or servers, keep all messages and shared content appropriate for all audiences (13+).

Do not share or post:

  • NSFW, violent, or disturbing content
  • Links to scams, malware, or phishing sites
  • Offensive usernames, bios, or images
  • Misinformation or fake news about Greek Games Studios or other users

5. Protect Your Account

Keep your account information private. We will never ask for your passwords or personal login data.

Be cautious of impersonators, only trust official staff members with verified roles or tags.

If you suspect someone is pretending to be Greek Games Studios staff, report it immediately.

6. Advertising and Spam

To keep our spaces clean and focused, the following are not allowed:

  • Excessive self-promotion or advertisements
  • Repeated or off-topic messages (spam)
  • Sharing Discord invites or links without permission

If you want to collaborate, promote an event, or share content related to our games, please contact a staff member first.

7. Reporting and Moderation

Our moderation team monitors our games and community platforms to ensure a safe environment.

If you see behavior that breaks these guidelines:

  • Report it to moderators or administrators
  • Do not engage in arguments or retaliation

Moderators may issue:

  • Warnings
  • Mutes or temporary bans
  • Permanent bans (for severe or repeated violations)

All moderation decisions are made at our discretion.

8. Testing and Feedback

We often run testing phases or early access versions of our games. Players are encouraged to provide constructive feedback and bug reports.

However:

  • Do not share unreleased content or leaks
  • Do not use testing builds for exploits or unfair advantages

Your input helps us make our games better, please use it responsibly.

9. Discord and Other Platforms

Our Discord server follows both these Guidelines and the official Discord Terms of Service. Breaking Discord's own rules may lead to both server and platform-level penalties.

The same applies to other third-party platforms such as Roblox, Steam, or Google Play, you must also follow their respective terms and community standards.

10. Enforcement

Violations of these Guidelines may result in:

  • Warnings
  • Muting, kicking, or banning
  • Revocation of special roles or privileges
  • Reporting to the platform provider (e.g., Roblox, Discord)

We reserve the right to take moderation actions at our discretion to maintain a safe and fair community.

11. Updates to These Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines periodically to reflect changes in our games or community policies. All updates will be posted on our official website and Discord server. Continuing to use our Services after updates means you accept the revised rules.

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions, need help, or wish to report misconduct, please contact us via:

Email: greekgamesstudios@gmail.com
Discord: https://discord.gg/UmrwZpYZP3
