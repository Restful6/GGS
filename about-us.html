Our Story

2022

The Spark

Everything began with a dream in Thessaloniki. A small group of passionate teens, united by their love for gaming and creativity, started experimenting with Roblox development, driven purely by curiosity and ambition.

2023

First Steps

After months of learning and testing, the idea for Unlimited Roleplay was born - a realistic Greek-themed RP experience like no other on Roblox. The early builds showed great potential, and the team began expanding their vision.

2024

The Birth of Greek Games Studios

Greek Games Studios (GGS) was officially founded. With clear branding, a growing online presence, and multiple projects in development, the team shifted from casual creators to an organized indie studio. That same year, development also began on the mobile series Try Not To Rage, marking the teamâ€™s entry into 2D mobile gaming.

2025

Growth & Innovation

GGS launched its first internal AI system, Nexis, and began creating cinematic teasers, custom launchers, and cross-platform tools. The studio now balances development across Roblox, Android, and PC - with a strong focus on quality, community, and innovation. We are not a company but we are a small team with the dream to became a company.
