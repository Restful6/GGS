About Us

An ambitious and creative game development team from Thessaloniki, Greece. We build worlds that inspire, entertain and bring players together.

Our Identity

Who We Are

Greek Games Studios is a game development team based in Thessaloniki, Greece. Founded by a small group of developers with a shared interest in game design, we work on projects across Android and PC. We're still learning, still building and still figuring things out as we go.

Our projects span action games, platformers and simulation experiences. We try to put genuine care into everything we make. At GGS, we don't claim to be industry leaders, we're just a team that enjoys building worlds and hopes players enjoy spending time in them.

Our Mission

Our Goal

Innovation

We want to push the boundaries of game development by exploring new technology and bold ideas. Our goal is to craft experiences that challenge convention, even if we're still figuring out exactly how to get there.

Community

We want to build meaningful connections with our players listening, evolving, and creating games that truly resonate. Right now we're small, but we hope to grow a global community that genuinely cares about what we make.

Quality

We aim to deliver high-quality, polished games with meticulous attention to detail. Every release is a step toward that standard and we're working to close the gap between where we are and where we want to be.

Our Journey

The Story So Far

2022

The Beginning

A small group of teenagers in Thessaloniki, interested in technology and games, started experimenting with development tools. No one really knew what they were doing, but they had time, curiosity and the patience to figure things out as they went.

2023

First Challenges

The gaps in experience became obvious quickly. Projects fell through, deadlines were missed and coordination was messy. Some people left, others stayed. The ones who stuck around learned mostly from what went wrong.

2024

A New Identity

The group decided to get a bit more organized a name, a basic logo and a social media page. It still felt small and amateur, but it was the first time they looked like an actual studio instead of just a group chat making side projects.

2025

Growth & Vision

The team is still small, still learning and still working on its first real release. There's no guarantee any of this will work out, but they keep showing up, keep building and keep hoping that consistency will eventually matter more than luck.

2026

Still Here

The team shrank to just a few people, but they finally shipped their first games. Downloads were modest and reviews were mixed, but they did okay for a first attempt. Updates are slow and progress is uneven, but they're still building and still showing up.
