About Us

Who We Are

Greek Games Studios is a creative and ambitious game development team based in Thessaloniki, Greece. Founded by passionate young developers with a shared vision, we specialize in crafting unique gaming experiences across platforms like Roblox, Android, and PC.

From immersive roleplay worlds to fast-paced 2D challenges, our projects blend innovation, detail, and heart. At GGS, we don't just make games we build worlds that inspire, entertain, and bring players together.

Our Goal

Innovation

We strive to push the boundaries of game development by leveraging cutting-edge technology and bold ideas, crafting experiences that challenge convention.

Community

We are committed to building meaningful connections with our players — listening, evolving, and creating games that truly resonate with our global audience.

Quality

We deliver high-quality, polished games with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring every release meets the highest standards.

Our Story

2022

The Beginning

A small group of teenagers in Thessaloniki, united by their passion for technology and creativity, began experimenting with development. With no prior knowledge but plenty of imagination, everything started as a simple dream.

2023

First Challenges

Real challenges soon appeared: lack of experience, limited time, and difficulties in coordination. Still, the team never gave up. Every setback became a lesson, and each small success gave them motivation to keep moving forward.

2024

A New Identity

The group decided to organize and build a clear identity. They created a name, a logo, and their first online presence. What once was just a casual circle of friends evolved into an indie studio with vision and ambition.

2025

Growth & Vision

Today, the team continues to grow and evolve, exploring new ideas and technologies. They are not yet a company — but a small community driven by passion, creativity, and the belief that with hard work, their dream can one day become reality.
